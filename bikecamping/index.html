Bike Camping
LACS Bike Camping Club
Bike camping is an activity in which the a group gathers all provisions necesary to survive for a few days, straps it to bikes, and lives in the woods for a day or two. The only rules are that cars are banned, that littering is forbidden, and you must be able to bike to join.

LACS's bike club was founded by Julius Won and Leo Scott; to join, talk to one of us. Be prepared to eat some variety of vegetable stew.
